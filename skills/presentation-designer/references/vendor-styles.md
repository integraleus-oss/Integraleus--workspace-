# Vendor Brand Styles Reference

## International Vendors

### Siemens
- **Style**: Strict German minimalism
- **Palette**: Teal `#009999`, dark gray, white
- **Visual**: Clean lines, lots of whitespace, isometric infographics, gradients from teal to dark blue
- **Photos**: Real facilities, production, people in hard hats
- **Font**: Siemens Sans family

### AVEVA / Schneider Electric
- **Style**: Modern, energetic, digital-first
- **Palette**: Schneider green `#3DCD58`, dark blue, white
- **Visual**: Digital twins, 3D visualizations, software screenshots, gradient backgrounds, semi-transparent overlays

### Honeywell
- **Style**: Corporate restraint with red accent
- **Palette**: Red `#E10600`, dark gray `#333333`, white
- **Visual**: Dark backgrounds with glowing elements (neon lines, data points) for Connected Enterprise
- **Focus**: Reliability and scale

### Emerson / AspenTech
- **Style**: Balanced — technical but accessible
- **Palette**: Purple `#7B2D8E`, green accent (post-AspenTech merger)
- **Visual**: Process diagrams, P&ID-style visuals, flow charts

### ABB
- **Style**: Swiss precision
- **Palette**: Red `#FF000F`, white, gray
- **Visual**: Precise grids, clear typography, abstract tech patterns, robots, drives, electrical equipment

### Yokogawa
- **Style**: Japanese aesthetic — restrained, futuristic
- **Palette**: Blue shades, lots of white space
- **Visual**: Data-centric, charts, graphs. "Co-innovating tomorrow" philosophy

### Rockwell Automation
- **Style**: American industrial — pragmatic, results-oriented
- **Palette**: Red `#C8102E`, gray
- **Visual**: Product photos, specific case studies, concrete numbers

## Russian Vendors

### Атомик Софт (detailed — extracted from real presentations)
- **Domain**: SCADA/HMI/АСУ ТП — Альфа платформа (Alpha Platform)
- **Theme name**: "Custom 2"
- **Color scheme**:
  - dk1: `#000000` (black text)
  - dk2: `#6E6E6E` (gray secondary text)
  - lt1: `#FFFFFF` (white)
  - lt2: `#FFFFFF` (white)
  - accent1: `#005497` (deep blue — primary brand color)
  - accent2: `#3375AD` (medium blue)
  - accent3: `#0997C8` (teal/cyan — links, highlights)
  - accent4: `#52CCF7` (light cyan)
  - accent5: `#82C444` (green)
  - accent6: `#CDE7B4` (light green)
  - hlink: `#0997C8`
  - folHlink: `#CDE7B4`
- **Additional text colors found in slides**:
  - `#00559A` (darker blue, used for headings)
  - `#11A0D7` (bright cyan)
  - `#445469` (dark slate — body text)
- **Fonts**:
  - Major (headings): Arial
  - Minor (body): Arial
  - Accent font: Circe ExtraBold (used for company name, large titles)
- **Font sizes**: 11–40pt range; titles 28–40pt, body 11–18pt, metrics 26–34pt
- **Slide size**: 10.00 × 5.62 in (standard 16:9 at lower res)
- **Layouts used**: Helium_Break_2 (title), General Slide (content), No Footer (special), Master Slide 1 (logos grid), Quotes, 1_Contact Us (final)
- **Visual style**:
  - Clean, structured engineering look
  - Groups of icon-blocks for metrics (e.g. "120+ сотрудников", "6500+ инсталляций")
  - Photos of industrial facilities as backgrounds with overlays
  - Logo grids for partners/customers
  - Two-tone approach: dark blue headers, white body
- **Key content blocks**:
  - Company overview with metric tiles
  - Awards/ratings slide
  - Value proposition (Независимость/Единая платформа/Функциональность/ИБ)
  - Platform capabilities
  - Architecture diagrams
  - Project case studies (Транснефть ВСТО-II, НОВАТЭК Арктик СПГ 2, Сочинская ТЭС, СИБУР, СберСити, ВОЛМА)
  - Partners (R&D / Технологические / Интеграторы 80+)
  - Customer logos grid
  - Contacts (Томск, пр. Ленина 60/1)
- **Focus**: Import substitution, Astra Linux / РЕД ОС compatibility, ФСТЭК certification
- **Style**: Strict, engineering-oriented, blue-dominant

### IEC Digital
- **Domain**: Industrial digitalization, IIoT, MES
- **Palette**: Dark backgrounds with bright accents (teal, green)
- **Visual**: Dashboards, real-time charts, digital-native
- **Focus**: IT/OT integration, big data, predictive analytics

### Реглаб (RegLab)
- **Domain**: APCS software, regulation and modeling
- **Palette**: Blue-white with gray tones
- **Visual**: Functional block diagrams, regulation diagrams, transient process graphs
- **Style**: Academic-engineering, restrained

### ТРЭИ (TREI)
- **Domain**: Industrial automation, integration, telemechanics
- **Palette**: Blue, orange accent, gray
- **Visual**: Structural diagrams of automation objects, control cabinet photos, SCADA screens
- **Focus**: Reliability, certification, Russian standards compliance

### Инкомсистем (Incomsystem)
- **Domain**: APCS, MES, EAM system integrator
- **Palette**: Dark blue, white, steel gray
- **Visual**: Integration architecture diagrams, ISA-95 level diagrams, industrial facility photos
- **Focus**: Turnkey solutions, project experience

### Прософт-Системы
- **Domain**: Controllers, telemechanics (Regul, ARIS series)
- **Palette**: Blue, gray, white
- **Visual**: Product photos of controllers and I/O modules, wiring diagrams
- **Focus**: Industrial reliability, Russian origin

### ОВЕН (OWEN)
- **Domain**: Largest Russian instrumentation manufacturer
- **Palette**: Orange (brand color), white
- **Visual**: Product catalogs, real equipment photos
- **Style**: Marketing-oriented, accessible to broad engineering audience

### Элеси (Elesy)
- **Domain**: APCS hardware-software complexes (oil & gas, energy)
- **Palette**: Blue-gray
- **Visual**: Architecture diagrams, real facility photos
- **Focus**: Russian certifications, critical infrastructure solutions
